Digital marketing is the use of online platforms, tools, and strategies to promote products or services. It includes techniques like search engine optimization (SEO), content marketing, social media marketing, pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, email marketing, and affiliate marketing. Unlike traditional marketing, digital marketing allows businesses to reach a global audience with targeted advertising and real-time engagement.

Digital marketing is essential because it provides businesses with a cost-effective way to reach their target audience, build brand awareness, and increase sales. Unlike traditional marketing, which can be expensive and limited in reach, digital marketing allows businesses to target specific demographics, track performance in real-time, and optimize campaigns for better results. It also enables businesses to engage with customers directly through social media, email, and other online channels.

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of improving a website’s visibility on search engines like Google. It involves optimizing website content, structure, and backlinks to increase rankings for relevant keywords.

There are two main types of SEO:
On-Page SEO: Includes keyword optimization, meta tags, internal linking, and high-quality content.

Off-Page SEO: Focuses on building backlinks from authoritative websites to improve domain authority.

SEO is a long-term strategy that helps businesses attract organic (free) traffic and improve online credibility.

Organic marketing refers to unpaid strategies like SEO, social media engagement, and content marketing, which help build brand awareness over time. Paid marketing, on the other hand, includes PPC ads, display ads, and sponsored social media posts, which provide immediate visibility but require a budget. While organic marketing builds trust and authority, paid marketing delivers faster results and allows precise audience targeting.
